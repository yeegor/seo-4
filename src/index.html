The best rat recipe of 2020!

This is a recipe of the famous Riga-style rat!

Ingredients:

  • 1 rat
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cigar
  • 1/8 bottle vodka
  • 1/8 bottle of beer
  • 1 lemon

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 250 degrees C. Grease a pan.
  2. Skin the rat. Take the bones out of the meat.
  3. Whilst the oven is getting ready, marinate the rat in an antiseptic liquid for 10 minutes.
  4. Wash the antiseptic liquid out of the rat.
  5. Mix beer with vodka. Marinate the rat in this marinade for 10 minutes.
  6. Cut the cigar. Roll the rat in tobacco leaves so that they stick to it. We use tobacco instead of the pepper.
  7. Pour the juice of the lemon onto the rat. Add salt and eggs.
  8. Put the rat into the oven for 45 minutes.
  9. Your rat is ready. Bon appetit!

Enjoy your Riga-style rat right after completing these simple steps! Bon appetit!
